About Us

Uche Ventures Nigeria Ltd is a leading service provider in Enugu State, Nigeria, specializing in air conditioning repairs and installation, refrigerator repairs and servicing, and a wide range of electrical services. With years of experience and a strong commitment to excellence, we have built a solid reputation for delivering reliable, efficient, and affordable technical solutions to both residential and commercial clients.

Located at Mbanefo Street, behind Mount Carmel Hospital and Mortuary, Upper Chime, New Haven, Enugu, we are strategically positioned to serve customers across Enugu and its surrounding areas with speed and professionalism.

Our team consists of highly skilled and certified technicians who are passionate about solving problems and ensuring the comfort, safety, and satisfaction of our clients. Whether you need a simple repair, a complex installation, or regular maintenance, Uche Ventures is equipped to handle it with precision and care.

At Uche Ventures Nigeria Ltd, we believe in quality workmanship, honest service, and long-term customer relationships. Our goal is not just to fix your equipment—but to provide peace of mind through dependable service you can trust.
